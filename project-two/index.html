Type: Multi-Culture Center in downtown Lubbock, TX.

The Fall 2023 studio study has been engaged to submit a proposal for the Lla- no Estacado Migration and Cultural Center- a point of arrival and departure in Downtown Lubbock that celebrates movement across the South Plains (hu- man, nonhuman, Indigenous, historic, and comtemporary) while welcoming travelers to the “Hub City.“

We articuled a vision for a complex program that integrates art, observation, and procession.
